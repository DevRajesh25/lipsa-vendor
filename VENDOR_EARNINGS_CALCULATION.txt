VENDOR EARNINGS CALCULATION SYSTEM
===================================

CALCULATION FORMULA:
-------------------
For a product with price ₹900:

1. Product Price: ₹900 (base price)
2. Tax (18% GST): ₹162 (calculated on product price)
3. Total Amount: ₹1062 (customer pays this = product + tax)
4. Commission (10%): ₹90 (calculated on product price only, NOT on tax)
5. Vendor Earnings: ₹810 (product price - commission)

KEY POINTS:
-----------
✓ Tax is calculated on the product price (18% GST)
✓ Customer pays: Product Price + Tax
✓ Commission (10%) is calculated on product price only, NOT including tax
✓ Vendor earnings = Product Price - Commission
✓ Tax goes to government, not included in vendor earnings or commission

IMPLEMENTATION:
---------------
1. Order Creation (app/api/orders/route.ts):
   - Calculates vendor earnings per vendor
   - Stores in vendorEarnings[vendorId] field
   - Stores commission in vendorCommissions[vendorId] field
   - Properly rounds all amounts to 2 decimal places

2. Dashboard (services/dashboardService.ts):
   - Shows total earnings from vendorEarnings field
   - Only counts earnings from paid orders
   - Calculates pending payout (earnings not yet paid out)

3. Earnings Page (app/(vendor)/vendor/earnings/page.tsx):
   - Displays total earnings per vendor
   - Shows commission breakdown
   - Lists pending payouts

4. Order Totals (lib/utils/orderUtils.ts):
   - Calculates 18% GST on subtotal
   - Adds tax to get total amount customer pays

EXAMPLE ORDER:
--------------
{
  "totalAmount": 1062,      // Customer pays (includes tax)
  "productPrice": 900,      // Base product price
  "tax": 162,               // 18% GST
  "commission": 90,         // 10% of product price
  "vendorAmount": 810,      // Vendor earnings (product - commission)
  "paymentStatus": "paid",
  "orderStatus": "completed",
  "payoutStatus": "pending",
  "vendorId": "vendor_1"
}
